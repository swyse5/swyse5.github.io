About Me

Work

I am currently a senior at Miami University, where I study computer science. Following graduation in May, I will begin working at Vantiv in Cincinnati in the Future Leaders Development Program - Technology.

During the summer of 2016, I was a software engineer intern at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) near Cincinnati, Ohio. Throughout this internship, I was apart of a team that developed a web application from the ground up. On the client side of this application, we used React-Redux, along with HTML and CSS. On the server side, we used Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Mongoose. My role on this team was primarily to develop back end REST APIs. I also stepped into the role of doing quality assurance for our application. To keep the project on task and collaborative, we used scrum methodologies and Git. At the end of the summer, we were able to deliver a working application to our client.

In the summer of 2015, I interned as a software engineer at Cloud Elements in Denver, Colorado. In this work, I developed RESTful APIs while working in an agile environment. I also had the opportunity to learn Python while working with Selenium WebDriver.

Languages: CSS, HTML, Java, JavaScript, Python, and SQL

Software and Technical Experiences: AJAX, APIs, Database Systems, Express, Git, Mac OS, MongoDB, MySQL, Node.js, PHP MyAdmin, React-Redux, RethinkDB, Selenium WebDriver, and Windows

Knowledge Areas: Agile Methodologies, Scrum, and the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
