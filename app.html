🎯 How Best Ball Works

During each round of the 4 Majors, your 4 golfers compete as a team. On each hole, only your best score counts.

Example: Your 4 golfers score bogey, par, birdie, bogey on hole #1. Your team gets a birdie! 🎉

Your team's total score across all 4 rounds determines your standing.

👥 Picking Your Team

📅 Two Lineups Per Major

  • Rounds 1-2: Submit before Thursday tee times
  • Rounds 3-4: Submit before Saturday tee times

After cuts, you can change all 4 golfers, keep the same, or mix and match!

💵 Salary Cap Rules

  • Total lineup value cannot exceed $100
  • Values update before each major
  • Minimum golfer value: $15
  • No carrying over unused cap between lineups
⚠️ If you don't submit a weekend lineup, your Round 1-2 golfers carry over. Anyone who missed the cut = empty roster spot!

💸 The Bogey Pot

If your team records a bogey or worse on any hole (all 4 golfers bogey+), you owe $5 to the pot.

At season's end, the top 3 players with the most eagles split the pot:

🥇 50% 🥈 30% 🥉 20%

Eagle tiebreakers: Most albatrosses → Most hole-in-ones

📋 Other Rules

🏥 Withdrawals

If a golfer withdraws before their tee time, you may replace them with any golfer of equal or lesser value before that new golfer tees off.

If they WD mid-round, you can replace them for the next day but not the current round.

It's YOUR responsibility to track your golfers' status!

🚨 Emergency Backup

If you're unsure a golfer will make their tee time, text the commissioner a "backup" golfer who will only enter your lineup if the original can't play.
