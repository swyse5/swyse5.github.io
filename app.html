How Best Ball Works

  • Pick 4 Golfers: Select 4 golfers from the field within the salary cap
  • Best Score Counts: On each hole, your score is the best (lowest) score among your 4 golfers
  • 18 Holes × 4 Rounds: Best ball scoring applies to all 72 holes
  • Lowest Wins: The player with the lowest total best ball score wins!
